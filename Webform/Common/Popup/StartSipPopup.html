Systematic Investment Plan is a convenient way to make investments towards long term goals. With SIP, you can invest a fixed amount every month for up to 15 years. Your money is debited automatically from your bank account through ECS mandate provided by you. Units are allocated based on NAV (Net Asset Value) applicable for the day.

A lumpsum purchase is a convenient option when you wish to invest certain amount of accumulated savings. It is done with a single payment and payment can be done immediately through Net Banking or Debit card.

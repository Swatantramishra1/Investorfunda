Corporate Fixed Deposits

Enjoy an ascertained rate of interest on your investments by investing in Fixed Deposits of private companies. Offering the same features as bank FDs, corporate FDs assure that you just have to sit back and enjoy the returns. Credit Ratings - Credit ratings mirror the credibility of the particular company. The higher the credit rating, the higher you can trust. Evaluate FDs on this factor and make a wise decision. Repayment History - History of a company can be used as a torchbearer before making an investment. Go through the repayment history of the company and give yourself a mental satisfaction before investing. Company Background - Assess the background of the company and take a serious consideration of the same. Background check may include financials, stability and the functioning of the company.
